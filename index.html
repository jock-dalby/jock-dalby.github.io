A little about me....

Hi everyone! My name's Jock, I live in Wellington, NZ and I am training to become a web developer.

My background is managing pubs, bars and restaurants across the UK, Australia and New Zealand. In May 2015, I left the hospitality industry in search of a new challenge and a better quality of life.

Soon after, myself and two fellow co-founders headed to Christchurch to take part in Lightning Lab, 2015, NZâ€™s premiere business accelerator programme. Over the 12-weeks at LL,CHCH we founded the company MightyGem.com, a customer feedback, loyalty and rewards system for cafes, bars and restaurants.

Lightning Lab gave me a real insight into the web industry and I quickly discovered a passion for technology, innovation and entrepreneurship. Since leaving the lab I have been studying hard to increase my knowledge & understanding, and have completed several courses on Codecademy and Treehouse to develop my technical skills.

I am now ready to accelerate my learning, and to kickstart a new career in web development. Hospitality and Lightning Lab both involved long hours and creative thinking, all while working in an intensive, high-pressure environment. This is the kind of environment in which I thrive, and what really attracted me to Dev Academy.

Over the coming 18 weeks I will be recording my experiences through a series of blogposts. If anyone has any questions, insights or feedback, it would be great to hear from you.

Otherwise, bon voyage and wish me luck!
