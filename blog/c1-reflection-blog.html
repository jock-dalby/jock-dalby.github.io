First impressions of Dev Academy, Wellington

Hey there, thanks for tuning in.......

So, here we are on Day-2 of phase-0 at Enspiral Dev Academy, Wellington.
Yesterday we had a brief orientation session via zoom, where I had chance to meet one of the tutors and a few of my fellow cohortee's and then in the evening our first weeks information and assignments were delivered. Included in said assignements was a section outlining the culture at EDA and felt this would be a great topic on which to begin my blog.

Overall I am feeling excited, optimistic and inspired by the whole EDA experience. I have spent the past few months completing online courses on Codecademy and Treehouse and am really looking forward to putting the skills I have learned into action.

My background is managing pubs, bars and restaurants, but left the industry early last year to pursue a life of entrepreneurship. Almost immediately, I found myself along with two other co-founders heading down to Christchurch to attend the 12 week business accelerator, Lightning Lab.
Both hospitality and accelerator programs are high-pressure, intensive working and learning environments, that involve long hours and require you to think on your feet; this is what I love about the EDA program as opposed to say a degree etc.; it's quick, it's fast and it delivers results. Also, at LL,CHCH I learned a great deal about tech, innovation and web development; all three of which I have discovered a great passion for.
